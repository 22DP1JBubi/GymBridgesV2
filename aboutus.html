About us

Description of the idea and the problem:

Fitness users often lack consistency and a convenient way to access information to help them achieve their fitness goals. Information can be dispersed across different fitness apps and platforms, and many people want a convenient solution that brings together multiple features in one place. The problem is that people can feel confused when looking for the right exercise information, exercise programmes, nutrition and workout locations.

The solution with GymBridges:

GymBridges is an integrated fitness platform that brings together different fitness functions in one app, offering a comprehensive and user-friendly solution. With this app, people will have easier access to information on exercises, workout programmes, nutrition and the nearest fitness centres, providing a convenient and motivating experience.

Goals:

  • Centralised information: Providing easy access to all the fitness information you need in one place.

  • Personalisation: Offer personalised workout programmes and nutritional advice based on the user's individual needs and goals.

  • Motivation: The opportunity to set and cover fitness goals, and get feedback and support from the community.

  • Offer cooperation: Allows users and personal trainers to collaborate, offer and receive personal training programmes.

Target audience:

GymBridges are designed for people of all ages and fitness levels looking for a convenient and effective way to achieve their fitness goals.
The target audience includes:

  • Beginners who want to start their fitness journey.

  • Experienced athletes looking for new challenges and professional support.

  • People with busy lifestyles who want to optimise their fitness experience.

  • Personal trainers who want to offer their services and attract clients.
